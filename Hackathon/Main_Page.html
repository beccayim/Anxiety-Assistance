About

Anxiety Assistance's goal is to offer assistance for people
who suffer from anxiety. We wanted to make an enjoyable website for
anxiety users to use as well as to assist them in a crisis

Did You Know?

Anxiety is the most common mental health issue.
1 in 4 adults suffer from anxiety.
